Filterable Gallery ?

Do you have a lot of photos that you would like to display in an organized manner??..If yes, filterable galary is the solution you are looking for. Filterable Gallery functionality allows you to group your photos into categories. Once the photos are grouped in categories, you can use them as clickable filters on the front-end. This exciting image gallery widget comes with a lot of styling options, features, and makes it so easy to build powerful galleries on your Elementor website.
